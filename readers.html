Ministers of the Word

Back

One of the important parts of the mass is the Liturgy of the Word. The readings from the Bible are important for Catholics as it allows God to talk to us. With this, readers are needed so the Word of God could be conveyed to the congregation.

If you would like to become a reader, please contact the parish office or attend the Mass at which you would like to read at.
