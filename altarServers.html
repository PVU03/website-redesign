Ministers of the Altar

Back

The Sacred Heart Parish altar servers are a diverse team of all ages who provide a liturgical ministry for assistance at all parish masses. Generally, candidates are eligible to be trained as altar servers after receiving their first communion. Young adults and men are especially encouraged to serve at the altar, in the hope that some may consider vocations to the priesthood.

The parish altar serving program encourages the highest standards of Catholic liturgy, to encourage the community's participation in a fruitful worship of God that is reverent and prayerful. By being part of a positive team working together for the glory of God, altar servers also develop fellowship with other ministries, learning that work at the altar is prayer in action.

Please contact the parish office or attend the Mass at which you would like to serve as an altar server.
